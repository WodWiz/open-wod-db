# Whitten

Five rounds of a five-movement circuit — kettlebell swings, box jumps, a 400m run, burpees, and wall-ball shots — all at 22 reps.

**5 rounds for time**
  22 Kettlebell Swing (72lb/53lb)
  22 Box Jump
  Run 400m
  22 Burpee
  22 Wall Ball Shot (20lb/14lb)

*Type: Interval · Category: Hero*
