# Bradshaw

Ten rounds of a short combo — 3 handstand push-ups, 6 deadlifts, 12 pull-ups, and 24 double-unders — repeated as a fixed circuit rather than building further.

**10 rounds for time**
  3 Handstand Push Up
  6 Deadlift (225lb/155lb)
  12 Pull Up
  24 Double Under
