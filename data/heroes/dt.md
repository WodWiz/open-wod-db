# DT

Five rounds of a descending 12-9-6 barbell triplet — deadlift, hang power clean, and push jerk — all at the same fixed weight, so the bar never leaves the hands between movements within a round.

**5 rounds for time**
  12 Deadlift (155lb/105lb)
  9 Hang Power Clean (155lb/105lb)
  6 Push Jerk (155lb/105lb)

*Type: Interval · Category: Hero*
