# Monti

Five rounds of two loaded barbell step-up sets bookending a clean set and a snatch set — a step-up-heavy circuit built around two different Olympic lifts.

**5 rounds for time**
  50 Barbell Step Up (45lb/35lb)
  15 Clean (135lb/95lb)
  50 Barbell Step Up (45lb/35lb)
  10 Snatch (135lb/95lb)

*Type: Interval · Category: Hero*
