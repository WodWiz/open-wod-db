# Pheezy

Three rounds of a six-movement circuit alternating a heavy low-rep barbell lift with a higher-rep bodyweight movement, three times over.

**3 rounds for time**
  5 Front Squat (165lb/115lb)
  18 Pull Up
  5 Deadlift (225lb/155lb)
  18 Toes To Bar
  5 Push Jerk (165lb/115lb)
  18 Hand Release Push Up

*Type: Interval · Category: Hero*
