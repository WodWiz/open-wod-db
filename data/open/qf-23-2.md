# 23.QF2

A 12-minute AMRAP of dumbbell snatches and overhead lunges split evenly between arms, finishing each round with crossover single-unders.

**AMRAP 12 min**
  8 Dumbbell Snatch (70lb/50lb)
  8 Dumbbell Overhead Lunge (70lb/50lb)
  8 Dumbbell Snatch (70lb/50lb)
  8 Dumbbell Overhead Lunge (70lb/50lb)
  40 Crossover Single Under

*Type: Amrap · Category: Quarterfinal*
