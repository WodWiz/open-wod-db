# 26.QF1

A mirrored workout of shuttle runs, overhead squats, and burpees over the bar, performed forward and then back in reverse order.

**For Time**
  10 Shuttle Run
  20 Overhead Squat (115lb/80lb)
  30 Burpee Over The Bar
  30 Burpee Over The Bar
  20 Overhead Squat (115lb/80lb)
  10 Shuttle Run

*Type: For Time · Category: Quarterfinal*
