# 19.4

A two-part workout: three rounds of snatches and bar-facing burpees, followed by three rounds of bar muscle-ups and bar-facing burpees.

**Part 1**
**3 rounds for time**
  10 Snatch (95lb/65lb)
  12 Bar Facing Burpee
  *rest 3 min*

**Part 2**
**3 rounds for time**
  10 Bar Muscle Up
  12 Bar Facing Burpee

*Type: Multi Part · Category: Open*
