# 12.2

A 10-minute ascending-load snatch ladder — three fixed sets of 30 reps at increasingly heavy weights, then max reps at the heaviest load for the remainder of the time cap.

**AMRAP 10 min**
  30 Snatch (75lb/45lb)
  30 Snatch (135lb/75lb)
  30 Snatch (165lb/100lb)
  max Snatch (210lb/120lb)

*Type: Amrap · Category: Open*
