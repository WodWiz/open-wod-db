# 18.3

Two rounds of a four-movement circuit — overhead squats, ring muscle-ups, dumbbell snatches, and bar muscle-ups — with 100 double-unders buffering every movement.

**2 rounds for time**
  100 Double Under
  20 Overhead Squat (115lb/80lb)
  100 Double Under
  12 Ring Muscle Up
  100 Double Under
  20 Dumbbell Snatch (50lb/35lb)
  100 Double Under
  12 Bar Muscle Up

*Type: Interval · Category: Open*
