# 26.2

Three rounds of a dumbbell lunge-and-snatch pairing, each round finishing with a harder pull-up variation — regular, then chest-to-bar, then muscle-up.

**For Time**
  Dumbbell Overhead Lunge 24m (50lb/35lb)
  20 Alternating Dumbbell Snatch (50lb/35lb)
  20 Pull Up
  Dumbbell Overhead Lunge 24m (50lb/35lb)
  20 Alternating Dumbbell Snatch (50lb/35lb)
  20 Chest To Bar Pull Up
  Dumbbell Overhead Lunge 24m (50lb/35lb)
  20 Alternating Dumbbell Snatch (50lb/35lb)
  20 Muscle Up

*Type: For Time · Category: Open*
