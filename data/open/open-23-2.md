# 23.2

A two-part workout: an ascending-rep AMRAP of burpee pull-ups and shuttle runs, followed by a separately scored one-rep-max thruster taken from the floor.

**23.2A**
**AMRAP 15 min**
  5 Burpee Pull Up
  10 Shuttle Run

**23.2B**
**Max load**
  1 Thruster

*Type: Multi Part · Category: Open*
