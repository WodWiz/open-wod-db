# 21.QF1

A two-part workout: three rounds of strict handstand push-ups, dumbbell hang power cleans, and double-unders, followed by three more rounds swapping in regular handstand push-ups and dumbbell shoulder-to-overhead.

**Part 1**
**3 rounds for time**
  10 Strict Handstand Push Up
  10 Dumbbell Hang Power Clean (50lb/35lb)
  50 Double Under
  *rest 1 min*

**Part 2**
**3 rounds for time**
  10 Handstand Push Up
  10 Dumbbell Shoulder To Overhead (50lb/35lb)
  50 Double Under

*Type: Multi Part · Category: Quarterfinal*
