# 17.2

A 12-minute AMRAP alternating a dumbbell-lunge, toes-to-bar, and power-clean round with a dumbbell-lunge, bar-muscle-up, and power-clean round.

**AMRAP 12 min**
  Dumbbell Walking Lunge 15m (50lb/35lb)
  16 Toes To Bar
  8 Power Clean
  Dumbbell Walking Lunge 15m (50lb/35lb)
  16 Bar Muscle Up
  8 Power Clean

*Type: Amrap · Category: Open*
