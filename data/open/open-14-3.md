# 14.3

An 8-minute workout pairing box jumps with a deadlift ladder that gets heavier at every step (10 reps up through 35, the barbell adding weight each time) rather than lighter.

**AMRAP 8 min**
  10 Deadlift (135lb/95lb)
  15 Box Jump
  15 Deadlift (185lb/135lb)
  15 Box Jump
  20 Deadlift (225lb/155lb)
  15 Box Jump
  25 Deadlift (275lb/185lb)
  15 Box Jump
  30 Deadlift (315lb/205lb)
  15 Box Jump
  35 Deadlift (365lb/225lb)
  15 Box Jump

*Type: Amrap · Category: Open*
