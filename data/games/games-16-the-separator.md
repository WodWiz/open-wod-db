# The Separator

Three rounds trading a descending ring handstand push-up count for an ascending squat variation — back squat, then front squat, then overhead squat — with burpees between each.

**For Time**
  12 Ring Handstand Push Up
  15 Back Squat (225lb/165lb)
  20 Burpee
  9 Ring Handstand Push Up
  18 Front Squat (205lb/145lb)
  20 Burpee
  6 Ring Handstand Push Up
  21 Overhead Squat (185lb/125lb)
  20 Burpee

*Type: For Time · Category: Games*
