# The End 2

The same eight-movement circuit as The End 1, extended to a 6-minute AMRAP.

**AMRAP 6 min**
  20 Row
  30 Wall Ball Shot (20lb/14lb)
  20 Toes To Bar
  30 Box Jump
  20 Kettlebell Sumo Deadlift High Pull (108lb/72lb)
  30 Burpee
  20 Shoulder To Overhead (135lb/95lb)
  Sled Pull
