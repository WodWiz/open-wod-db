# Inverted Medley

A mirrored gymnastics medley of handstand walks, freestanding handstand push-ups, and obstacle pirouettes that rotate a full 180 and then 360 degrees.

**For Time**
  Handstand Walk 9m
  8 Freestanding Handstand Push Up
  Obstacle Pirouette
  16 Pull Over
  Obstacle Pirouette
  8 Freestanding Handstand Push Up
  Handstand Walk 9m

*Type: For Time · Category: Games*
