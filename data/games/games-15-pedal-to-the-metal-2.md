# Pedal to the Metal 2

A companion event to Pedal to the Metal 1, swapping in parallette handstand push-ups and a heavy kettlebell deadlift for the pegboard and dumbbell snatch.

**For Time**
  12 Parallette Handstand Push Up
  24 Row
  16 Bike
  8 Kettlebell Deadlift (203lb/124lb)
