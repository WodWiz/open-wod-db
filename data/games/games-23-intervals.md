# Intervals

Two mirrored intervals of box jump-overs, rowing calories, and burpee box jump-overs, the movement order flipped between the two.

**Interval 1**
**For Time**
  21 Box Jump Over
  15 Row
  9 Burpee Box Jump Over
  21 Box Jump Over
  15 Row
  9 Burpee Box Jump Over

**Interval 2 (starts at the 6:00 mark)**
**For Time**
  9 Burpee Box Jump Over
  15 Row
  21 Box Jump Over
  9 Burpee Box Jump Over
  15 Row
  21 Box Jump Over

*Type: Multi Part · Category: Games*
