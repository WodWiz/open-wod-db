# Split Triplet

Five rounds of a pegboard ascent, double-unders, and a dumbbell hang split snatch immediately followed by a split-style dumbbell hang clean and jerk.

**5 rounds for time**
  1 Pegboard
  100 Double Under
  10 Dumbbell Hang Split Snatch (80lb/55lb)
  10 Dumbbell Hang Clean And Jerk (80lb/55lb)

*Type: Interval · Category: Games*
