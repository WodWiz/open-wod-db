# 2007 (2013 Games)

A 1000m row followed by five rounds of pull-ups and push jerks — a near-identical repeat of the 2007 Games' Hopper Workout, referencing that inaugural year directly in its name.

**For Time**

  25 Pull Up

  25 Pull Up
