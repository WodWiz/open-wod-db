# Maggie

Five rounds of handstand push-ups, pull-ups, and alternating pistols at escalating per-movement volume — a gymnastics-heavy sibling of Mary, compressed into a fixed five-round interval rather than an open AMRAP.

**5 rounds for time**
  20 Handstand Push Up
  40 Pull Up
  60 Pistol

*Type: Interval · Category: Girl*
